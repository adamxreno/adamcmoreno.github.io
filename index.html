I am Adam C. Moreno. I am a news communicator and educator focused on politics, internet culture, and the ideas that shape our future. I am the founder of Paper, a media news company that uses storytelling, humor, and analysis to report what’s happening to millions across platforms.

I create videos and write about news, politics, internet culture, and big ideas — with a focus on helping people make sense of what’s actually happening beneath the noise.

I’m the host of The Adam C. Moreno Show, where I break down political moments, media narratives, and cultural flashpoints in a way that’s curious and human. My work is less about telling people what to think, and more about slowing the conversation down enough to think clearly.

Alongside the show, I write a newsletter exploring current events, ideas about the future, and reflections on creativity, technology, and more.
