Hello. I’m Adam C. Moreno.

I am a news communicator and educator focused on politics, internet culture, and the ideas that shape our future. I am the founder of Paper, a media company that uses storytelling, humor, and analysis to report what’s happening to millions across platforms.

I create videos and write about news, politics, internet culture, and big ideas — with a focus on helping people make sense of what’s actually happening beneath the noise.

I’m the host of The Adam C. Moreno Show, where I break down political moments, media narratives, and cultural flashpoints in a way that’s curious and human. My work is less about telling people what to think, and more about slowing the conversation down enough to think clearly.

I care about how people experience the world — especially as internet culture, media, and technology increasingly shape how we talk, argue, and understand one another.

Alongside the show, I write a newsletter exploring current events, ideas about the future, and reflections on creativity, responsibility, and being human in a chaotic moment.

Current projects

  • The Adam C. Moreno Show — daily long-form videos focused on news, politics, and cultural moments
  • Adam’s Newsletter — essays, quick takes, and big ideas delivered straight to your inbox
  • ATOMS — short, straightforward news summaries designed to cut through jargon and outrage
  • The Adam C. Moreno Show (Bites) — short-form versions of the show for people scrolling
  • Battleground — a paid membership for deeper conversations and long-form discussion

What I believe

  • the world is complicated, but not incomprehensible
  • curiosity is more powerful than outrage
  • most people are trying to live decent lives inside broken systems
  • good conversations matter — especially when we disagree

I’m not interested in talking points or partisan theater. I’m interested in understanding what’s real, what’s being missed, and what kind of future we’re quietly building.
